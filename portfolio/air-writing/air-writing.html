Info

Air Writing is a computer vision application that transforms hand gestures into digital drawings. This innovative tool uses your webcam to track hand movements and allows you to draw in the air without physical touch interfaces. The application uses MediaPipe's hand tracking technology to detect and track hand landmarks in real-time.

The Challenge

  • Accurately tracking hand movements in different lighting conditions
  • Creating smooth drawing lines from gesture movements
  • Designing intuitive gesture controls for drawing and erasing

The Solution

  • Implemented MediaPipe's hand tracking for robust finger position detection
  • Created a smoothing algorithm for natural-looking drawing strokes
  • Developed simple gesture controls using index finger and thumb positions
