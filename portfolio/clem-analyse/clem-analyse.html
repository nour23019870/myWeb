Info

CLEM is an advanced health analysis tool that uses computer vision and AI to assess health indicators through facial and body analysis. The system captures real-time video, analyzes facial features, body posture, and provides detailed health assessments and recommendations. It's designed to help users monitor their physical health and wellness metrics in real-time through non-invasive computer vision analysis.

The Challenge

  • Accurately detecting subtle facial and body health indicators from video input
  • Processing complex health metrics in real-time with low latency
  • Translating technical measurements into meaningful health insights and recommendations

The Solution

  • Integrated facial landmark detection with advanced body posture analysis algorithms
  • Implemented GPU acceleration for real-time processing of complex health metrics
  • Developed comprehensive reporting system with personalized health recommendations
