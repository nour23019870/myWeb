Info

L1ght Music Downloader is a sleek command-line tool for downloading audio from YouTube videos and playlists in MP3 format. The application features a stylish ASCII art interface with visual progress bars, automatic conversion to high-quality MP3 format, and organized file saving directly to your Music directory. It provides a simple yet powerful way to build your music collection from YouTube content.

The Challenge

  • Creating a user-friendly command-line interface for audio extraction
  • Managing reliable downloads from both individual videos and playlists
  • Ensuring high audio quality conversion with proper metadata

The Solution

  • Designed an intuitive ASCII-art interface with progress visualization
  • Leveraged the yt-dlp library for reliable and efficient downloads
  • Implemented automatic format conversion with optimized quality settings
