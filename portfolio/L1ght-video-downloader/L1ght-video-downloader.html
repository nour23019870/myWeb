Info

L1ght Video Downloader is a sleek, terminal-based YouTube video downloader that combines style with functionality. It downloads videos from YouTube in the highest available quality, with a visually appealing interface featuring animated progress bars and elegant ASCII art. The tool downloads the best video and audio streams separately, then merges them to ensure maximum quality, all while providing a seamless user experience with real-time progress indicators.

The Challenge

  • Obtaining maximum quality video that exceeds typical single-stream limitations
  • Creating an intuitive terminal interface that's both functional and visually appealing
  • Managing the complex process of downloading, merging, and cleaning up temporary files

The Solution

  • Implemented separate best-quality audio and video stream downloading with automated merging
  • Designed a stylish ASCII art interface with animated progress indicators
  • Bundled FFmpeg with the application for seamless media processing without additional dependencies
